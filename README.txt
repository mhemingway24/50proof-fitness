DROP YOUR PHOTO HERE
====================

To display your photo in the hero section, add your image to this folder and name it:

    hero-photo.jpg

The site will automatically display it. Any standard image format works
(JPG, PNG, WEBP) — just rename it to hero-photo.jpg.

Tips:
- Portrait orientation (taller than wide) looks best
- Minimum 600px wide recommended
- The image fills the frame and is cropped to fit (object-fit: cover)
